About

Momeato is a pure vegetarian restaurant in Ahmedabad built around honest cooking, warm service and the comfort of freshly prepared food. Since 2010, the restaurant has focused on serving dishes that feel generous, flavorful, and made with care.

What makes Momeato special is its home-style spirit. The kitchen is led by talented housewives who bring experience, patience, and a personal touch to every plate. The goal is simple: serve quality food in a space where families and friends love to return.

Since Fresh vegetarian dining since 2010.
Hours Open daily, 10:00 AM to 11:00 PM.
